# QUESTS.md - Quest Lines & Objectives

## Main Quest Line

### Chapter 1: Getting Established
**Objective:** Meet 3 suppliers and make your first $1,000
- Talk to suppliers in each territory
- Buy product from any supplier
- Sell to customers and earn initial capital
- **Reward:** Unlock "Connected" status, access to runners

### Chapter 2: Building the Network
**Objective:** Earn $10,000 and unlock Tier 3 safehouse
- Expand operations to 3+ territories
- Hire your first runner
- Maintain heat below 50% to avoid police crackdown
- **Reward:** Tier 3 safehouse (Apartment), faster production

### Chapter 3: The Faction Wars
**Objective:** Choose a faction alliance, earn $50,000
- Complete 3 missions for your chosen faction
- Gain territory in their stronghold
- Manage relationships (ally/enemy factions)
- **Reward:** Faction headquarters, special discounts

### Chapter 4: Taking Over The Docks
**Objective:** Earn $500,000 and control 50% of territories
- Eliminate rival faction leaders (optional)
- Complete final faction quest
- Build empire across all territories
- **Reward:** Victory! You're the new King of The Docks

---

## Win Conditions

### Victory
- Accumulate $500,000 total net worth
- Control 50%+ of all 12 territories

### Peaceful Victory  
- Reach $500K without eliminating any faction leaders
- All faction relationships at "Allied" status

### Dominance Victory
- Eliminate all 12 faction leaders
- Control 80%+ territories
- Total underworld control

## Side Quests (Per Faction)

### The Old Family (The Don)
- **Delivery:** Bring 10 product to their warehouse
- **Protection:** Defend their shipment from rival
- **Intelligence:** Gather info on The Crown's operations

### The Serpents (Viper)
- **Sample Testing:** Test new product on customers
- **Competition:** Drive out a street dealer
- **Research:** Find rare chemical ingredients

### The Crown (Rook)
- **Gambling Setup:** Establish a poker game location
- **Debts:** Collect from a deadbeat customer
- **Cheater:** Deal with someone cheating at cards

### The Ravens (Ghost)
- **Elimination:** Remove a target (optional kill)
- **Surveillance:** Photograph a rival meeting
- **Extraction:** Rescue a captured Raven member

### The Iron Hands (Iron)
- **Vehicle:** Acquire a specific vehicle
- **Parts:** Gather rare car parts
- **Repair:** Fix a vehicle for a client

### The Jackals (Fang)
- **Scavenge:** Find valuable scrap from the docks
- **Smuggling:** Move product in hidden compartments
- **Territory:** Claim an abandoned building

### The Drift (Storm)
- **Hit-and-Run:** Disrupt a rival's operation
- **Getaway:** Provide escape route for a job
- **Recruitment:** Bring new members to the gang

### The Shadows (Shade)
- **Acquisition:** Obtain specific black market item
- **Fence:** Sell hot merchandise
- **Network:** Expand their hidden trade routes

### The Embers (Blaze)
- **Supply Run:** Source chemical precursors
- **Production:** Assist in cooking operation
- **Distribution:** Set up new selling spots

### The Ice (Frost)
- **Smuggling:** Move illegal cargo through docks
- **Bribes:** Arrange payoff for customs officials
- **Storage:** Guard a frozen storage facility

### The Blades (Razor)
- **Arms Deal:** Complete a weapon transaction
- **Protection:** Guard a safehouse
- **Weapon Acquisition:** Find specific firearms

### The Network (Byte)
- **Hack:** Breach a rival's communications
- **Surveillance:** Install hidden cameras
- **Data:** Recover lost encrypted files

## Reputation System

- **0-25:** Unknown / Street Level
- **26-50:** Known / Connected  
- **51-75:** Trusted / Respected
- **76-100:** Legendary / King of The Docks

Higher reputation unlocks:
- Better supplier prices
- Exclusive equipment
- Bigger orders
- Faction leadership opportunities
