# Equipment System

## Overview
The Trap game features a comprehensive equipment system that allows players to upgrade their capabilities across multiple categories: storage, weapons, armor, utility, and accessories.

---

## Storage

### Backpack
- **Cost:** $200
- **Type:** Storage
- **Effect:** +5 Raw Material capacity, +3 Product capacity
- **Description:** Carry more Raw Materials

---

## Weapons

### Brass Knuckles
- **Cost:** $150
- **Type:** Weapon
- **Effect:** +2 Attack (street confrontations, rival encounters)
- **Description:** Fight better in street confrontations

### Switchblade
- **Cost:** $350
- **Type:** Weapon
- **Effect:** +4 Attack
- **Description:** Deadly in close-quarters combat

### Pistol
- **Cost:** $800
- **Type:** Weapon
- **Effect:** +8 Attack, enables ranged attacks
- **Ammo Cost:** $25 per shot
- **Description:** Ranged weapon. Requires ammo to use.

---

## Armor/Defense

### Bulletproof Vest
- **Cost:** $500
- **Type:** Armor
- **Effect:** 30% damage reduction from police encounters
- **Description:** Reduces damage from police encounters

### Heavy Coat
- **Cost:** $250
- **Type:** Armor
- **Effect:** 25% reduction in heat gain
- **Description:** Reduces heat gain from exposure

### Designer Sunglasses
- **Cost:** $350
- **Type:** Accessory

- **Description:** Reduces police detection chance

---

## Equipment Effects in Gameplay

### Combat (Weapons)
- Attack bonus applies to rival encounters and self-defense
- Pistol enables ranged attacks (costs ammo per shot)
- Multiple weapons stack their attack bonuses

### Defense (Armor)
- Bulletproof Vest reduces damage taken from police when caught
- Heavy Coat reduces heat gained from police encounters and exposure

### Movement (Utility)
- Running Shoes increases grid movement speed by 50%
- Binoculars expands the visible area on the minimap
- Lockpick reduces the time needed to enter safehouses
- Burner Phone reduces the cooldown between buyer spawns

### Economy (Accessories)
- Gold Chain adds 10% to all sale prices from buyers
- Designer Sunglasses makes it harder for police to spot you

---

## Strategy Tips

1. **Early Game:** Prioritize Backpack for more carrying capacity, then Running Shoes for mobility
2. **Mid Game:** Add Brass Knuckles for combat, Heavy Coat for heat management
3. **Late Game:** Gold Chain for profit maximization, Bulletproof Vest for safety
4. **Specialized Builds:**
   - Combat: Switchblade + Pistol + Bulletproof Vest
   - Speed: Running Shoes + Lockpick + Burner Phone
   - Stealth: Designer Sunglasses + Heavy Coat + Binoculars
   - Profit: Gold Chain + Backpack
