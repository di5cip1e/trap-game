## GAMEPLAY TIPS

1. **Early Game:** Light armor, melee weapon, focus on stealth
2. **Mid Game:** Medium armor, pistol, build reputation
3. **Late Game:** Heavy armor, automatic weapons, control territory

**Equipment affects:**
- Combat effectiveness
- Heat generation
- NPC reactions
- Shop prices
- Mission options
