# Tomothy's Goals

## Primary Goal
Design and implement the complete geography of The Docks - every street, building, and hidden corner.

## Current Objectives

1. **Map Expansion**
   - Add more distinct neighborhoods to The Docks
   - Create memorable Points of Interest
   - Design faction territory boundaries

2. **Spatial Storytelling**
   - Ensure every area has environmental narrative
   - Place visual clues about faction history
   - Create "discovery" moments for players

3. **World Integration**
   - Connect map to quest system
   - Link locations to faction storylines
   - Make exploration rewarding

## Success Metrics
- Player can navigate The Docks intuitively
- Each area feels unique and memorable
- Exploration reveals story and rewards
