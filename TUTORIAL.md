# TRAP - Game Tutorial

*A survival guide for the streets. Read this, or become another statistic.*

---

## 1. MOVEMENT

*The streets belong to those who know how to navigate them.*

**Controls:**
- **WASD Keys** - Move up, left, down, right
- **Arrow Keys** - Alternative movement controls
- **Touch/Click** - Tap anywhere on the map to walk there

**Tips:**
- Move strategically. Each step costs time and hustle.
- Stick to alleyways and side streets - less heat, more speed.
- Know when to run and when to walk. Running burns hustle fast.

---

## 2. MAKING MONEY

*This is a business. Raw materials → Processing → Product → Cash. Master the cycle or get crushed.*

### The Three Steps:

**Step 1: Buy Raw Materials**
- Find a **Supplier** (marked with 📦 on the map)
- Purchase raw materials - they cost $50 per unit
- Watch your inventory limit!

**Step 2: Process (Cook)**
- Go to your **Workstation** (marked with 🔥 in Safehouse)
- Convert raw materials into product
- Processing costs HUSTLE - keep an eye on your energy bar
- Yield: 2 units of product per 1 raw material

**Step 3: Sell**
- Find a **Buyer** (marked with 💵 on the map)
- Sell your product for $100 per unit
- Each sale generates HEAT - police don't like entrepreneurs

**Key Formula:**
```
Profit per unit = $100 (sell) - $50 (buy) - hustle cost
Net: ~$85 profit per cycle, minus heat consequences
```

---

## 3. SAFEHOUSE

*Your base of operations. Upgrade it, or die in the streets.*

### Features:

**Sleep (Recover)**
- Rest to recover HUSTLE (energy)
- Sleeping removes HEAT
- Different safehouse tiers offer different benefits

**Stash Items**
- Store raw materials and product for later
- Prevents losing everything if you get arrested
- More stash slots as you upgrade

**Workstation**
- Process raw materials into sellable product
- Upgrade to process faster and more efficiently

**Safehouse Tiers:**
- **Crib** (Tier 1): Basic, 2 stash slots
- **Shack** (Tier 2): Small upgrades, 4 stash slots
- **Apartment** (Tier 3): Workstation unlocked
- **Townhouse** (Tier 4): Better security, 8 stash slots
- **House** (Tier 5): Can hire runners, 10 stash slots
- **Warehouse** (Tier 6): Large operations, 15 stash slots
- **Mansion** (Tier 7): Full capabilities, 20 stash slots

---

## 4. HEAT & POLICE

*The law is always watching. Stay below the radar or face the consequences.*

### Understanding Heat:

- **0-25%**: Low heat - police ignore you
- **26-50%**: Medium heat - increased police spawns
- **51-75%**: High heat - police actively hunt you
- **76-100%**: Critical - CONSTANT pursuit

### Heat Sources:
| Action | Heat Gain |
|--------|-----------|
| Selling product | +5 per sale |
| Passing out with product | +25 |
| Running from police | +10 |
| Winning a fight | +5 |

### Reducing Heat:
- **Sleep at Safehouse**: -20 heat per sleep
- **Natural decay**: -10 heat per new day
- **Time**: Patience pays off

### Police Encounters:
- If heat exceeds 50%, police may spawn
- Police chase you if spotted
- Get far enough away to lose them
- If caught, you lose all product and pay a fine

---

## 5. RUNNERS (Tier 3+)

*Work smarter, not harder. Let others do the dirty work.*

### What Runners Do:
- Automatically sell product while you're away
- No heat generated from their sales
- They take a percentage, but the volume makes up for it

### How to Hire:
- Unlock at Safehouse Tier 3 (House)
- Visit the Safehouse menu
- Pay the hiring fee
- Assign product to your runner

### Tips:
- Stock your runner with product before sleeping
- Higher-tier runners work faster and keep more of the profit
- Build a network for maximum automation

---

## 6. CALENDAR & WEEKLY EVENTS

*The streets change every week. Adapt or get left behind.*

### Time System:
- Each day has morning, afternoon, evening, and night phases
- Certain locations only open at specific times
- Weekends bring special opportunities

### Weekly Events (Every Week 7 Days):

**🔴 CRACKDOWN**
- Police everywhere
- Heat builds faster
- Stay low, limit sales
- *Tip: Stockpile product before, sell during peak times*

**🟢 DROUGHT**
- Product prices sky rocket
- Buyers pay 2x-3x normal
- Supply is limited
- *Tip: Buy extra raw materials before, profit big*

**⚡ POWER OUTAGE**
- Some locations close
- Workstations slow down
- Safehouse remains operational
- *Tip: Plan processing ahead of time*

## 7. COMBAT (RPS BATTLE)

*Sometimes you can't run. Sometimes you have to fight.*

### Rock-Paper-Scissors Combat:
The battle system is simple but strategic:

| Your Choice | Beats | Loses To |
|-------------|-------|----------|
| 🔨 Hammer (Strength) | ✂️ Knife | 🛡️ Shield |
| ✂️ Knife (Speed) | 🛡️ Shield | 🔨 Hammer |
| 🛡️ Shield (Defense) | 🔨 Hammer | ✂️ Knife |

### How Combat Works:
1. Both fighters pick simultaneously
2. Winner deals damage based on ability stat
3. Luck can trigger critical hits
4. First to 0 HP loses

### Combat Tips:
- Read your opponent (sometimes patterns emerge)
- High INTUITION helps predict moves
- ABILITY increases damage dealt
- LUCK can save you from bad choices
- Don't fight fair - run if you're outmatched

### After Combat:
- Win: Gain respect, minor heat, potential loot
- Lose: Lose product, pay medical bills, lose heat from trauma
